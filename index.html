Audio Samples from "Sequence-to-Sequence Voice Conversion Based on Limited Parallel Data"

Paper:

Sequence-to-Sequence Voice Conversion Based on Limited Parallel Data (Submitted to National Conference on Man-Machine Speech Communication, NCMMSC 2019)

Abstract:

For the case where the source and target speakers have a small amount of parallel data and a large amount of non-parallel data, this paper proposes a sequence-to-sequence voice conversion method based on a small amount of parallel data. First, by using the small amount of parallel data, an initial model for voice conversion is pre-trained; Then, pseudo-parallel data is generated by converting non-parallel data using the pre-trained model to upgrade the model parameter. Finally, the model parameter estimation is completed by the iterative generation of pseudo-parallel data. The experiment results show that the proposed method effective-ly improve both the naturalness and similarity of converted speech comparing with the model only using a small amount of par-allel data, and the performance of proposed method is close to the sequence-to-sequence conversion model trained with the same amount of parallel data in subjective test.
